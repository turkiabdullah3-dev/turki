About Cosmic Gravity Explorer

Project Description

Cosmic Gravity Explorer is an interactive visualization experience for understanding black holes and wormholes through modern web graphics.

It presents key general relativity ideas in a visual and intuitive way, helping learners explore spacetime behavior such as time dilation, redshift, and curved geometry.

Scientific Basis

  • General Relativity: spacetime curvature produced by mass and energy.
  • Black Hole Model: Schwarzschild solution for non-rotating, spherically symmetric mass.
  • Wormhole Model: Morris-Thorne traversable geometry with throat conditions and exotic matter requirements.

This project is an educational visualization layer built on accepted formulas, optimized for real-time interaction and conceptual learning.
